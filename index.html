Project Alpha

AI-powered UX tool redesign — making machine intelligence feel human.

UX Research AI Redesign
Role UX Lead
Timeline 6 months
Tools Figma, Maze, Hotjar

the problem

Users found the existing AI tool intimidating and opaque. Trust was low, task completion was lower. The interface communicated "here is our technology" instead of "here is your answer."

the approach

Led discovery with 14 user interviews across three personas. Reframed the product around outcomes rather than capabilities. Introduced progressive disclosure so power features never overwhelmed first-time users.

the outcome

Task success rate up 38%. Time-to-value dropped from 4.2 minutes to 90 seconds. NPS moved from +12 to +54 in three months post-launch.

Project Beta

End-to-end product design for a B2B SaaS platform trusted by 200+ companies.

Product Design B2B SaaS Systems
Role Product Designer
Timeline 9 months
Tools Figma, Notion, FullStory

the problem

A growing B2B platform had accumulated six years of UX debt. Each new feature added friction. Onboarding churn was 40% in the first week.

the approach

Ran a full audit: 200+ screens, 40 jobs-to-be-done, 8 critical flows. Rebuilt the information architecture from scratch. Designed a new onboarding arc that matched users' mental models rather than the product's internal logic.

the outcome

First-week churn dropped to 18%. Power user activation (reaching 5+ sessions) up 61%. Sales team reported shorter demo cycles because the product "finally explained itself."

Project Gamma

Design system & component library — the invisible foundation for everything.

Design Systems Tokens Component Library
Role UX Lead / Design Systems
Timeline 12 months
Tools Figma, Storybook, Tokens Studio

the problem

Seven product teams. Seven inconsistent button styles. Engineers rebuilding the same modal from scratch every sprint. The product looked and felt different depending on which team had shipped the screen.

the approach

Audited 900 components across 4 products. Defined a token system (120 semantic tokens) before touching a single component. Built in Figma with Storybook parity so design and code stayed in sync by default.

the outcome

Shipped 140 production-ready components in 12 months. New feature design time down 55%. Cross-team consistency score (internal UX audit) up from 3.1 to 8.8 out of 10.
